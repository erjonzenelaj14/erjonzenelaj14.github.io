Policy Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

At Vibzi, we are committed to protecting our users, especially minors, from any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have a zero-tolerance policy for any content, behavior, or user activity that promotes, enables, or involves CSAE.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Uploading or sharing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming, solicitation, or predatory behavior involving minors
  • Depicting or referencing minors in sexual or exploitative contexts
  • Any attempt to contact minors for inappropriate purposes

Moderation and Reporting

Vibzi uses both automated and manual content moderation tools to detect and remove harmful content. Users can report inappropriate content directly through the app. All reports are reviewed promptly.

Cooperation with Authorities

We fully cooperate with international law enforcement and child protection agencies. CSAE content will be reported to appropriate authorities, and accounts involved will be permanently banned.

Contact Us

If you encounter any suspicious or abusive behavior, please contact our Safety Team at: safety@vibzi.app
